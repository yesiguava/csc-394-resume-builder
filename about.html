About Us

Welcome to our website, where you can create your own online resume that is custom tailored to be the best possible fit for any job posting you link. Our website is designed to make the job application process easier and more efficient for you.

How It Works

To get started, simply register for an account. Once you've registered, you can enter in all of your skills and qualifications that you would include in your resume.

When you find a job posting that you're interested in, simply enter the URL link for that job posting on our website. Our website will then scrape the job posting to retrieve the essential qualifications and skills that would best fit that specific job posting and generate your custom resume.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the job application process easier for job seekers. We understand that job hunting can be a daunting task, so we created this website to help streamline the process and give job seekers an edge in their search for employment.

Our Team

Our team is experienced in the fields of software development, design, and customer service. Each member of our team is passionate about making the job application process easier for job seekers, and we work together to continually improve our website and services to better serve our users
